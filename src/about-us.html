About ImportEse

About Jiang Jing (Elsa)

I'm Jiang Jing (Elsa), the founder of Importese, graduated from Guangdong University of Foreign Studies, one of the Top 3 Universities in the field of English Translation and Interpretation in China.

In the beginning, I was a trade assistant, which enabled me to travel throughout China visiting all kinds of factories and companies together with my clients. It presents a perfect opportunity to study the work flow of various industries, communicate with all kinds of suppliers and build relations with them. It also helps me gain a better understanding of my clients, most of whom are e-commerce sellers. They tell me stories and pitfalls they face during importing processes. Perhaps you also feel that it's difficult to source an ideal supplier, to place an order at a good price, to manage suppliers so that they can give priority to your order, and to make sure that the products' quality is good after you invest in so much energy and money. Maybe you also face issues like corruption, ethics, transparency, scams and fraud. You are not alone!

Why start Importese?

Anyone wanting to succeed in China has to deal with these potential issues. But once you figure out how to maneuver through this minefield, you open the door to unlimited business opportunity. We believe that Importing is not difficult, everyone can enjoy the processes of importing from China and other low-cost, manufacturing based countries. We want every one of our clients import at ease.
